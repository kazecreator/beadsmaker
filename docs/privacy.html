Privacy Policy

BeadsMaker does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

All pattern data, drafts, preferences, and other content you create are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data.

If you enable iCloud Sync, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is governed by Apple's privacy policy. You can disable iCloud Sync at any time from the Profile screen.

We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, crash reporting, or advertising services.

BeadsMaker does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app contains no ads and no links to external sites beyond the Feedback email.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

If you have any questions or concerns about privacy, please contact us at kazecreator@gmail.com.
