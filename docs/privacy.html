豆你玩隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 4 月 27 日

豆你玩是一款用于创作、预览、保存和导出拼豆图纸的 iPhone 应用。当前发布版本不提供社区、发布、举报或账号后端功能。

我们收集的信息

  • 本地图纸内容：你创建的图纸、草稿、标题和配色保存在设备本地。
  • 购买状态：应用使用 Apple StoreKit 处理一次性内购。支付信息由 Apple 处理；应用会在本机保存 Pro 权益状态。
  • 相册权限：当你选择保存成品 PNG 时，应用会请求添加到相册的权限。应用不会读取你的相册内容。

我们如何使用信息

  • 提供图纸创作、草稿保存、成品预览和 PNG 导出功能。
  • 维护本机 Pro 权益和草稿数量限制。
  • 改进应用稳定性和用户体验。

数据共享

我们不会出售你的个人信息，也不会将数据用于第三方广告追踪。当前发布版本不向自有服务器上传图纸或账号资料。内购由 Apple 处理。

数据保留和删除

本地草稿保存在你的设备上，删除应用可能会删除这些本地数据。你可以在应用内删除草稿。

儿童隐私

豆你玩不是专门面向儿童的应用。若你认为未成年人向我们提供了需要删除的信息，请联系我们。

联系我们

如需隐私咨询或支持，请通过 GitHub Issues 联系我们：https://github.com/kazecreator/bead-maker/issues

PixelBeads Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 27, 2026

PixelBeads is an iPhone app for creating, previewing, saving, and exporting fuse bead patterns. This release does not provide community, publishing, reporting, or backend account features.

Information We Collect

  • Local pattern content: patterns, drafts, titles, and palettes you create are stored locally on your device.
  • Purchase status: the app uses Apple StoreKit for a one-time in-app purchase. Payment information is handled by Apple; Pro entitlement state is stored locally.
  • Photo library permission: when you save a finished PNG, the app asks permission to add images to Photos. The app does not read your photo library.

How We Use Information

  • Provide pattern creation, draft saving, finished preview, and PNG export features.
  • Maintain local Pro entitlement and draft-limit behavior.
  • Improve app reliability and user experience.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information and do not use data for third-party advertising tracking. This release does not upload patterns or account profile data to our own servers. In-app purchases are processed by Apple.

Retention and Deletion

Local drafts are stored on your device and may be deleted if you remove the app. You can delete drafts inside the app.

Children

PixelBeads is not specifically directed to children. Contact us if you believe information from a minor should be removed.

Contact

For privacy questions or support, contact us via GitHub Issues: https://github.com/kazecreator/bead-maker/issues
